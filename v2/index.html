About the Company

NH & E Group Inc

NH & E Group Inc is a Korea-focused cosmetic sourcing and distribution company specializing in anti-aging skincare. We identify formulations aligned with current Korean dermatological research and coordinate their production and distribution through a private, inquiry-based model.

Our distribution is structured around private client relationships. We do not operate a public retail storefront. Inquiries are received by email, and pricing is discussed directly with each client based on volume and product selection.

Products are manufactured at commercial scale through established Korean OEM/ODM partners. Our role is product selection, sourcing coordination, and client distribution — not ownership or operation of manufacturing facilities.

"Research-informed selection. Commercial-scale production. Private client distribution."

Manufacturing & Sourcing Model

OEM/ODM
Manufacturing Partners

Produced through established Korean OEM/ODM manufacturing partners. NH & E Group Inc coordinates production requirements and quality specifications through partner facilities. All production is handled at the partner level.
